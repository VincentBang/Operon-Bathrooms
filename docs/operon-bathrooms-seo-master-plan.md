# Operon Bathrooms SEO Master Plan

## Executive SEO Verdict

The SEO research recommends a phased strategy. Operon Bathrooms should first own the Sydney bathroom quote-clarity niche instead of competing immediately for broad "bathroom renovation Sydney" traffic.

Competitors commonly target broad renovation pages, generic cost guides, calculators or fixed-price package language. The opportunity is to rank for planning estimates, quote review, waterproofing prompts, PC/provisional sum clarity, strata readiness and site-measure preparation.

## Keyword Strategy

Phase 1 targets high-intent long-tail searches:

- bathroom renovation cost Sydney
- bathroom renovation quote Sydney
- bathroom quote review
- Sydney bathroom renovation estimate
- bathroom renovation planning estimate
- bathroom renovation cost calculator Sydney
- apartment bathroom renovation Sydney
- ensuite renovation Sydney
- small bathroom renovation Sydney
- bathroom site measure Sydney
- bathroom renovation quote comparison
- bathroom renovation quote too high

The durable positioning is: Sydney bathroom renovation estimates and quote review before you commit.

## Phase 3 Local SEO Rules

Local pages are delayed until unique local content exists. Do not create thin suburb pages. Area content must include real local context such as strata patterns, access constraints, building age, parking/loading issues or apartment stock differences.

Initial candidates from the research:

- Eastern Suburbs bathroom renovation cost.
- Inner West bathroom renovation cost.

## Phase 4 Expansion Rules

Only compete for broad "bathroom renovation Sydney" terms after Phase 1 and Phase 2 pages have traffic, links and conversion data. Broad pages should point back to the quote wizard, quote review and cost guide.

## Bad-Fit Keyword Policy

Do not position acquisition content around:

- cheap bathroom renovation
- DIY waterproofing
- emergency bathroom repairs
- supply-only fixtures
- free final quote online
- legal advice
- unlicensed renovation
- handyman bathroom renovation

If these phrases appear, they must be framed as warnings, fit filters or safety boundaries, not as service offers.

## Internal Linking Strategy

- Homepage links to estimate, quote review, request review, site measure, cost guide and service pages.
- Quote result links to quote review, request review, site measure and cost guide.
- Quote review links to cost guide, request review and site measure.
- Cost guide links to quote wizard, quote review, site measure and service pages.
- Service pages link to quote, site measure and quote review where relevant.
- Guides hub links to quote review and the core CTA paths without indexing thin child pages.

## Measurement Plan

Track:

- Organic impressions and clicks by landing page.
- Average position for primary keyword clusters.
- Quote wizard starts and completions by source/UTM.
- Quote review submissions by source/UTM.
- Request review submissions by source/UTM.
- Site measure requests by source/UTM.
- Cost guide to quote wizard conversion.
- Quote wizard to quote review conversion.
- High-risk/high-value lead count.
- Bounce rate and time on page for key pages.
- Backlink growth and referring domains.

## Ranking and Conversion KPIs

- Phase 1 pages indexed with unique titles, descriptions, canonicals and one H1.
- No admin/API/debug routes indexed.
- No thin suburb or guide pages indexed.
- Organic clicks begin from cost, quote review and quote wizard clusters.
- Quote review and site measure CTA clicks are visible from Phase 1 pages.
- No public rate/margin leakage in indexed content.
